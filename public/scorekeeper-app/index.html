    Big, fast buttons for points, fouls, hits, goals and more. Score one-handed from the sideline.

  • Live public scoreboard

    Share one link. Fans watch the score, clock and play-by-play update in real time.

  • Automatic stats

Frequently asked questions

Is SportStream really free?

Yes — live scorekeeping and public scoreboards are free forever. Paid plans add extras like CSV export and multi-camera streaming.

Do fans need an app?

No. Fans open a link in any browser to watch the live scoreboard and play-by-play. Coaches can install the app to their home screen for quick access.

Which sports are supported?

Basketball, baseball, softball, soccer, volleyball and flag football, each with a sport-specific scoring interface.

Can more than one person keep score?

Each game has a scorekeeper, and admins on your team can take over or run other games at the same time.
